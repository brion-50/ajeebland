about this object

this is an analogue computer. it has inputs. the inputs here are all based on rotations. the input is one rotation; the output is the rotation of different cycles. there are 5 cycles in this object: — the solar cycle of 52 weeks. — a handcrank that turns the passage of time; it relates to the lunar cycle. — a cycle that governs the replenishment of the tissues. — the 99 attributes and characteristics associated with god. — and a cycle associated with my menstrual cycle. the gears contain the rations of the cycles. each spin of the handcrank turns the gears and the clock hands to show where in each cycle we are, and its relationship to one another.
